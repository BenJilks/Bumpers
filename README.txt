
-- Running --
Pre-build binaries are located in the 'bin' directory.

-- Controls --
General:
1 - In car camera
2 - Ground camera
3 - Free camera

Car:
Up - Accelerate
Down - Reverse
Left - Turn left
Right - Turn right

Free Camera:
W - Forward
S - Back
A - Left
D - Right
Space - Up
Left Shift - Down
